Good Commit Messages

In my journey of learning Web Development, I have realised that it is very important to write good commit messages when pushing your code to an open source platform.

And I will tell you why, keep reading.

Who does it help?

Everyone!

This practice helps everyone.Here's how-

  • It helps the contributors to determine what exactly was altered/created in this commit.
  • It helps visitors to understand how your project was made , what problems you faced and how you fixed them.
  • It helps your teammates(if any) to pinpoint the updates/alterations in the project and decide their next step accordingly.
  • And finally, it helps you! You can easily track all the changes you made , how and why you made them.

Bonus!

In addition to the other benefits, the practice of writting good commit messages will also earn you more stars (likes) on github (or any other platform that you use), because the more easily people understand your work , the better they like it.

It will also earn you more followers in turn.

See!
Who would have thought that they can become famous just by writting good commit messages?
